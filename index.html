Frequently Asked Questions

How does the claiming process work?

When you locate a found item in the database, click "Submit Claim Request". You must provide specific details (such as serial numbers, unique scratches, or files inside a laptop) to verify ownership. The campus security admin team will review your claim and update its status.

Where do I collect approved items?

Approved claims can be collected at the **Central Campus Security Desk** located in the Student Union building (Room 102), open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM. Please bring your student ID and a copy of the Claim Approval notification.

Who is allowed to verify claims?

Only authenticated Campus Security officers and platform administrators have elevated permissions to review, approve, or reject ownership claims.
